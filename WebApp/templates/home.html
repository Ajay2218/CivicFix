About CivicFix

CivicFix is a community-first platform for reporting civic issues and tracking their resolution in one place. Residents can quickly submit problems like potholes, sanitation delays, broken streetlights, and water supply disruptions, while local teams receive clear, organized reports that speed up action. By keeping updates transparent and accessible, CivicFix helps neighborhoods stay safer, cleaner, and better maintained—together.
